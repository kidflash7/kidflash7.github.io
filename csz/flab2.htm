G-Forms - Lab 2 - Make a Survey

Use Forms to make a Survey to gather information from your Group (see Gmail Lab2)
Open up GMail, click on google apps icon in upper right corner choose Forms App choose Blank.

Survey needs to have:

  • At least 10 Survey Questions
  • Have a Theme
  • Lots of different Question Types
    • Short Answer
    • Multiple Choice
    • Check Boxes
    • Dropdown
    • Linear Scale
    • Date, Time
