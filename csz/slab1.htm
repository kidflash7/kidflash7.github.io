Make a Purchase Order form for ordering items from any store of your choosing.

Spread Sheets are dynamic, that means if I change the value in certain cells other cells with math calculations automatically change, that's why you are using a spread sheet and not a doc.

Please refer to my Lab 1 Ex:
All colored cells need to be dynamic(math calculations, i.e. =F16*0.15)
Same colored cells have similar math calculations.

Please refer to my Lab 1 Ex: #(number)
Example 1: I want cell F5 to be # in cell A5 times # in cell E5 so in cell F5 I will type =A5*E5. The equal sign means it's a math calculation.
Example 2: I want cell F16 to be the sum of all cells from F5 thru F15 so in cell F16 I will type =F5+F6+F7+...+F15.
