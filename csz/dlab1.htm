Word Processing - Lab 1 Docs - Schedule

In your Google Drive make a new folder to store your Assignments in (e.g. Computer Skillz)

Go into the new folder you created. Click on Google Apps Icon in the upper right hand corner of the screen and choose Docs App. Click on Blank, where it says "Untitled document", upper left hand corner of screen, change it to "Your Last Name - What Period You Are In - Document Type 1", i.e. "Alexander - Per 7 - Docs1"

Assignment : You will make a Doc that displays your class schedule and then underneath this will display the regular bell schedule (Red and Gold) for the week. Things to consider for obtaining the best possible score:

  • Data is displayed in a table
  • Table elements are centered
  • Is color used but not over used
  • Keep number of pages to a Minimum
  • Is your Doc aesthetically pleasing
