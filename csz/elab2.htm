Email (Gmail) - Lab 2 - Group Email

Do a group GMail, must have at least 5 recipients.

Creating Da Group:
Go into Google Apps and choose Contacts Click on Create Label, enter a name for the Label/Group (i.e. My Homies). Once done choose Directory find your Homies and choose (more actions), go down and find the group name (label name) and click on it. It will put a check mark next to the group, that means they are in the group. Repeat this for the rest of your homies

Sending the Group GMail:
I like to send the EMail to myself and cc it to my group that way I see what I sent to the Group, so click the COMPOSE button in the To box type in your GMail address. Now click the Cc at the end of the To box. Type in the name of your group. Now type in the subject of your email in the subject box and finally type in your email message in the space below and click the SEND button. You be done.
