Gates of the Artic is the 2nd biggest national park in the country. It has a and acrage of 7,523,898 and it was established as a national park in December of 1980. The park is found in Alaska. Three of the most popular sites in Gates of the Artic are Boreal Mountain, Fridig Crags, Kobuk Wild River. About 10,000 people make it to Gates of the Arctic each year. Like the name of the park implies it sits entirely inside the Arctic Circle. The park does not have a single visitor center, road or campground.
