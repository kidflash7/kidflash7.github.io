Suppose you want to make an ORDERED LIST. Type in what's in blue and you get what's in black ...

< ol >
< li > Get Jiggly Wit It < /li >
< li > Jiggly Wit Da Get < /li >
< li > Wit Jiggly Get < /li >
< /ol >

  1. Get Jiggly Wit It
  2. Jiggly Wit Da Get
  3. Wit Jiggly Get

Suppose you want to make an UNORDERED LIST Type in what's in blue and you get what's in black ...
a list header <lh> is optional

< ul >
< lh > Fav-or-ite Sayin's < /lh >
< li > Get To Gettin' < /li >
< li > Stylin' and Profilin' < /li >
< li > Nothin' To it But to Do it < /li >
< li > Plan Your Work, Work Your Plan < /li >
< li > Don't Bite Off More Than You Can Chew < /li >
< /ul >

    Fav-or-ite Sayin's
  • Get To Gettin'
  • Stylin' and Profilin'
  • Nothin' To it But to Do it
  • Plan Your Work, Work Your Plan
  • Don't Bite Off More Than You Can Chew
