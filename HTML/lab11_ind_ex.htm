Divide your page into FRAMES page
"Index.htm"

< html >
< head > < title > .... < /title > < /head >
< frameset cols="20%,*" border=0 frameborder=0 >
< frame name=nav src="nav.htm" >
< frame name=main src="main.htm" >
< /frameset >
< /html >

In your Index page you declare all your frames. This page has two frames your menu button frame and the main frame. I made it like my Tutorial into columns, 20% of the page is taken up by "nav.htm" and the rest of the page is taken up by "main.htm". You could of done it by rows and you get a menu on top or the buttom like the blue menu bar.

You should call your Frame page "index.htm", because when you load your page on a server, by default your sever will load up "index.htm" as your starting page. All your Frame page does is bring up 2 webpages in different parts of the screen.
